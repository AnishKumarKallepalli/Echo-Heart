Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and all associated voice data, please provide your email address below. We'll process your request within 30 days as required by data protection regulations.

What happens when you delete your account?

  • All your personal information will be permanently deleted
  • All voice recordings and audio files will be permanently removed
  • Your account credentials will be deactivated
  • You will lose access to any preserved voice data
  • This action cannot be undone
